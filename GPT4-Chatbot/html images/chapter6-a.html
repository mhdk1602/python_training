Enhancing Chatbot Relevance with Fine-tuning

1. Importance of Fine-tuning

Fine-tuning GPT-4 with organizational documents and data improves accuracy and relevance in responses.

2. Preparing Your Documents

Collect and curate relevant documents like product manuals, FAQ sheets, and customer service transcripts.

3. The Fine-tuning Process

Upload documents to OpenAI and adjust fine-tuning parameters like learning rate and epoch count.

4. Integrating Fine-tuned Models

Integrate the custom model with your chatbot's backend and test extensively.

5. Monitoring and Updating

Regularly review interactions and update the model to maintain relevance.
